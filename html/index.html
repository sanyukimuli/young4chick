Welcome to Young4Chick, a youth-powered poultry initiative equipping the next generation of farmers with the resources, knowledge, and support they need to thrive! We’re building a vibrant ecosystem of young entrepreneurs aged 18–30 who are ready to transform poultry farming across Uganda.

Whether you're just curious or already raising your first chicks, Young4Chick is your launchpad for sustainable agricultural impact and economic empowerment!

About Us

Young4Chick was birthed out of a vision to bridge the gap between youth unemployment and agriculture by providing meaningful pathways into poultry farming. Founded by a youth MP passionate about rural innovation, the initiative connects young people with starter chicks, training, and ongoing mentorship.

Through collaboration with brooder managers, sales reps, and agricultural experts, we ensure every registered young farmer gets the support they need to grow from beginner to business-ready.

Chickstarter Kit

Chickstarter Kit: How to Get Started

Step 1: Register as a Young Farmer
To be eligible, you must be between 18–30 years old. Fill out the registration form and once verified, you're eligible to submit chick requests once every 4 months.

Step 2: Request for Chicks
Only registered farmers can request chicks. Fill in the chick request form stating the number of chicks (100 for starters, up to 500 for returning farmers), the type of chicks (Broilers or Layers), and your farmer status (Starter or Returning). Your request will be sent to the Brooder Manager for approval.

Step 3: Wait for Approval
The Brooder Manager checks available stock and approves valid requests. Once your request is approved, the system updates to reflect reduced stock and your request will be marked as "Ready for Pickup".

Step 4: Pick Up Chicks & Feed
All chicks cost UGX 1,650 each, regardless of type. Each farmer is also eligible for up to 2 bags of feed, which are payable after 2 months. Once you receive your chicks, a Sales Rep will confirm the handover and record it as a complete sale in the system!

This kit is your launchpad into the poultry world—designed to help you build sustainable wealth through farming, what are you waiting for?
